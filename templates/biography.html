Biography

When I was a kid

From birth to my teens

Born with a weak immune system, I'd get sick from ever so slightly being exposed to the outside world. Little me was kept inside the house where I'd learn to enjoy indoor activities. This was my life all the way up until my teens. There really isn't much to say.

Middle and High School Years

Until I graduated school basically

At this point in life most of my health problems had dissipated. I became a regular boy having a regular life, though still sticking to my comfort zone of the sedentary house holed mister mans. However, I'd get involved in big national academic competitions every year called the "Olympiads". I brought back a medal every time I was allowed to participate in them. I also developed interest in software and technology after growing up my entire life handling them.

University Life

A fresh start

Right after graduating from high school, I left my home country to pursue a higher education in Canada. Moving out to a place I have never once visited and had no acquaintances with was a huge change of scenery. The culture and the geography and just having to live by my own efforts; it's as if I started life anew. But despite all that, I have been enjoying myself every day. There's always new challenges to take on and overcome.
